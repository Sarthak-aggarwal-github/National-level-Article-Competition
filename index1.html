NATIONAL LEVEL POSTER COMPETITION


ON
ADVANCES IN COMPUTER SCIENCE, COMPUTATIONAL INTELLIGENCE, ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION

in association with MIET &
CSI Student Chapter

01 - 02 June, 2021

National level Certificates will be provided to 40% participants of total participants.

Registration and Submission Process

  1. Participant need to register in the portal REGISTER HERE Both individual and group entries are permitted subjected to maximum of two members in one group. for group entries , only one member of the group needs to register.
  2. It is mandatory to upload the Institute Id Card /Organization Id card to register in the competition.

Who can Participate?

The applicant must be an Engineering student/IT Professional.

Submission Status

* Submission status will be evaluated as follows:

QUALIFYING ROUND:

Based on the recieved short papers(max.three pages, as per the prescribed format) , the shortlisted participants will be notified via registered email and the website.

FINAL ROUND:

The shortlisted participants from qualifying round will be called for final presentation in virtual mode using Google meet in front of expert committee.

Note:


* The winners will be declared by the expert committee at the end of the competition.

Judgement criteria for Final Round


  1. The work will be judged by an expert committee based on the following criteria:
    Originality and Creativity (40%): 40%
    Relevance to real-world (30%): 30%
    Presentation (30%): 30%
  2. The selected team has to present the poster for nearly 10 minutes.
  3. The winner will be announced on the scheduled date
  4. The judge’s decision will be final.

NOTE -:


  1. National Level Certificate for all the selected participants.
  2. Prize money will be given to first three teams.

About the Institute

MIET, located in the National Capital Region, is one of the oldest self-financed Engineering Institutions in Uttar Pradesh and also one of the best Skill Enhancement Training centers. MIET is declared a STAR PERFORMER (an Academic Excellence Award by UPTU). Like a true leader, it has taken several initiatives in the field of technical education that are being followed by other Institutions across the state now. MIET has an intensive academic environment aimed at bringing out the best out of the students. It lays claim to the finest faculty in the region, offers the best laboratory infrastructure, excellent hostels, mess facilities and promotes several extra- curricular activities. It also has a well stocked library in the state with over 96,000 volumes and subscribes to a number of National and International journals.
